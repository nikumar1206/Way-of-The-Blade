Way of the Blade

Welcome to Way of the Blade, a game inspired by Japanese animation!


Player 1 can move using the "WAD" keys and "E" to attack!

Player 2 can move using the "Arrow Keys" and "Enter" to attack!

Player with the most health wins!

Use the "WAD" keys and "E" to attack and defeat the enemy boss

Hint: The enemy boss may do more damage, but can only attack you every 2 seconds!
